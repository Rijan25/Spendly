Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data Spendly collects, how we use it, and the choices you have over your information.

1. Data We Collect

We collect the information you provide directly when you create an account — your name, email address, and password. We also store the expense records you add, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you choose to enter.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — to display your expenses, generate summaries, and enable filtering. We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app for you.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell or share your personal data with third parties. We may use trusted infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) to operate the app, but these providers are contractually bound to keep your data confidential and may not use it for their own purposes.

5. Cookies

We use session cookies strictly to keep you logged in. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics cookies.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out through our support channels. We aim to respond to all enquiries within 5 business days.
